1. Site Name

Fortaleza for you: Fortaleza Chamber of Commerce (FCC) - Invest & Host

Justification: The new name, "Invest & Host," is highly action-oriented and specifically targets the business tourism sector (MICE). It signals that the site is the central resource for both attracting new business investment and facilitating large-scale events and conferences in the city.

2. Site Purpose

The Fortaleza Chamber of Commerce website will serve as the **primary digital platform for Business Tourism (MICE), promoting the city's infrastructure and strategic location for major events and investments.

Its core mission is to attract, facilitate, and support national and international companies looking to host conferences, exhibitions, or establish a business presence in the region. The site will offer detailed information on convention centers, logistics providers, and key economic data, turning Fortaleza into a hub for business growth.

Furthermore, it will provide essential networking tools and a comprehensive directory of member businesses that support the MICE industry (e.g., hotels, transportation, event planning).

4. Color Schema

The selected colors are based on the tropical environment of Fortaleza (deep blue ocean and golden sun) while adhering to WCAG AAA contrast requirements for text accessibility.

  • Primary Color (Deep Ocean Blue): #004d73. Used for main headings, footer background, and key structural elements.
  • Secondary/Accent Color (Golden Yellow): #ffb703. Used for Call-to-Action (CTA) buttons (e.g., "Join Now"), hover states, and key highlights.
  • Background Color (Off-White): #f8f9fa. Used for the main body and content cards to ensure maximum readability against the text.
  • Text Color (Dark Gray): #212529. Used for all body and paragraph text to provide excellent contrast against the off-white background.

5. Typography

We will use a modern and highly readable font combination to ensure a professional and clean user experience.

  • Headings (H1, H2, H3): 'Roboto', sans-serif. Used for all titles and section headers to convey a clear, modern feel.
  • Body Text and Paragraphs: 'Open Sans', sans-serif. Used for all main content, directory listings, and form labels, prioritizing high legibility for long-form content.

6. Wireframe Description

The wireframe design for the home page (landing page) is based on the Chamber of Commerce project example, ensuring responsiveness for mobile and desktop clients[cite: 29].

Desktop View Layout:

  • The **Header** includes the FCC Logo and the main navigation links (Home, Discover, Directory, Join) visible and horizontal.
  • A large, responsive **Hero Image** dominates the top, featuring a CTA button (Keynote Tickets).
  • The main content area is a **multi-column layout** where the Events Spotlight, Current Weather, and 3-Day Forecast appear in distinct blocks, often side-by-side.
  • Three **Business Spotlights** are displayed in a row at the bottom of the main content.

Mobile View Layout:

  • The **Header** utilizes a "Hamburger" icon to collapse the main navigation links, prioritizing screen space.
  • The **Hero Image** remains at the top, scaling down for width.
  • All content blocks (Events, Weather, Forecast) are **stacked vertically** in a single-column layout to ensure easy scrolling and readability on narrow screens[cite: 45].
  • Business Spotlights are also stacked vertically, one above the other.
